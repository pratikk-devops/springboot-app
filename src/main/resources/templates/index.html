Our Business Partners

ARNO

ARNO tools - outstanding competence for demanding machining tasks. Short or long turning, grooving, turning, drilling or milling: Whatever is required we have outstanding solutions. We have been developing tool systems for three generations that impress with their quality, durability and process reliability. With the right mix of experience and pioneering spirit, we ensure that our customers get the best out of their production with tailor-made tool systems, flexible tool management solutions and clever innovations

DUMMEL

The origin of the carbide tool factory Paul Dümmel GmbH goes back four generations. Our company was established in 1928. The development and production of high-quality solid carbide metal cutting tools for metal working applications such as turning and milling has been one of our priorities for over 70 years. We are one of the leading carbide tool manufacturers in the rapidly growing industrial segment of miniature applications. We regard our customers as partners - not just when it comes to selling, but already during the development and manufacture of our tools.Based on this attitude we are a long termed productivity partner to our customers. Together we have the ability to optimize machining and increase productivity.
